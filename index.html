----------- Brief Description of Divine Dwells -----------

Divine Dwells is a premier licensed real estate company in Sri Lanka with its own unique identity. As one of the flagship entities of Divine Dwells Holdings,
we have remained rooted to bridge the gap between aspiration and achievement based on the unshakable foundation of
uncompromising quality, complete customer satisfaction and unparalleled living experience.

Having begun with what was then Sri Lanka’s pioneer portfolio of property development, Divine Dwells, by means of strategic alliances and bold
approaches, has now become a beacon of empowerment to the masses, its services reaching grass root level entrepreneurs, high-level corporate
executives and the upper medium class.
